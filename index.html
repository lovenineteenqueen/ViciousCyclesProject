Vicious Cycles is a bike shop located in Raleigh North Carolina that specializes in selling Trek cruiser bikes, adventure road bikes, hybrids and mountain bikes. They also sell accessories that go hand in hand with biking such as helmets, lights, fanny packs, workout apparel and snack bars/energy drinks. They provide bike parts, custom work and endless knowledge.

Our mission is to provide you with more knowledge and excitement about bikes on repeat.

Contact us for bike repairs or if you have any questions.
